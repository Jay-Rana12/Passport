Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services and processes.

How long does a tourist visa take?
The processing time varies by country. Usually, it takes between 7 to 15 working days. Some countries offer express services that take 3-5 days.
What documents are required for a passport?
Generally, you need proof of identity (Aadhaar/PAN), proof of address (Utility bills/Voter ID), and proof of date of birth (Birth certificate/10th Marksheet).
Can I apply for a visa online?
Yes! Our portal allows you to submit your details and documents online. We handle the filing and appointment scheduling for you.
What is Tatkal passport service?
Tatkal is an express passport service provided by the government for urgent needs. It usually processes within 1-3 working days.
Is my payment secure?
Absolutely. We use industry-standard encryption and secure payment gateways to process all transactions. We do not store your card details.
